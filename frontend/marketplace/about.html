About Us

"We learn from our mistakes to improve, so you don't have to worry"

Origins
One day, we wanted server space for our own project we were doing, so we set out to do so. We couldn't find any website which was reliable to rent the type of server that we wanted. So after that experience, we wanted to ensure that nobody else would go through the same pain as us. We started with some few ideas, some failed, some worked out, but this is our final product. This is how our company DAWHostServices was born.
Present
This website has our hearts put into it. We want to sell the best experience possible when renting server space, in other words, fast, reliable and great . It is so that our servers are at a great price. So if you are thinking about renting some server space, look no further, because you will not find a better place! We have hundreds of companies giving the same services, so what does make us stand out from the competition? Our customer service and our range of configurations for different servers.
Future
We have plans to offer a wider range of servers. Because in this age of ever-changing technology, everyone has to adapt, and we are no exception. Our motto is Fail, Learn, Adapt and Success. Firstly, we will encounter problems from which we need to learn. After the fact, with our new founded knowledge we can adapt ourselves to provide a better service and succeed in our company goals, which is the satisfaction of our clients by renting ever-improving servers.
